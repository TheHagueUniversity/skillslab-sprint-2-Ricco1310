About RicoICT

RicoICT was formed by Riccardo Waardenburg in late 2014. In its relatively short existence a plethora of projects have been run. In the saturated and mature Dutch market client satisfaction comes first and foremost. In RicoICT we believe in the no cure no p(l)ay method of work. Only once the client has satisfactory results, will payment occur. All work presented by RicoICT will be of highest quality, hand tailored to the clients company.
